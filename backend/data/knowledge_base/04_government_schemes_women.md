**For**: All women

**For**: Everyone (great for women)

**Benefits**:
- Safe government-backed investment
- 5-year lock-in
- Tax deduction under Section 80C
- Interest: ~7% per year

---

**For**: Girl child welfare

- Education support
- Health benefits

## How to Choose the Right Scheme

### For Savings:
- **SSY**: Best for girl child's future
- **Mahila Samman**: Short-term savings
- **NSC**: Long-term safe investment

### For Business:
- **MUDRA**: Small loans without collateral
- **Stand-Up India**: Larger business loans
- **Mahila E-Haat**: Online selling platform

### For Education:
- **SSY**: Can withdraw for higher education
- **Beti Bachao Beti Padhao**: Education support
