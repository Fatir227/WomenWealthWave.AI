# Introduction to Finance (Beginner)

Finance is the study of how to manage money — earning, saving, investing and protecting wealth.

## Why It Matters for Women
- Supports independence
- Helps plan education, travel, family and retirement
- Reduces financial stress
- Helps avoid scams & fraud

## Basic Terms
- Income = Money coming in
- Expense = Money going out
- Assets = Things you own (gold, property, stocks)
- Liabilities = Amount you owe (loan, credit bills)
- Budget = Plan of spending & saving
