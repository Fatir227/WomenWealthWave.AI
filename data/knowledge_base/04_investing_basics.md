# Investing Basics (Beginner Friendly)

Investing means putting money into assets to grow over time.

## Benefits
- Beats inflation
- Builds long-term wealth
- Helps reach life goals (education, house)

## Popular Options
- FD (Fixed Deposit)
- RD (Recurring Deposit)
- Mutual Funds
- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
- Stocks
- Gold/Govt Bonds

## Rule
Start early ? benefit from compounding.
