# Budgeting Basics

Budgeting means planning how to use your income.  

## 50–30–20 Rule
- 50% ? Needs (rent, food, bills)
- 30% ? Wants (shopping, movies)
- 20% ? Savings/Investments

## Steps
1. Track monthly income
2. List expenses
3. Categorize
4. Reduce unnecessary spending
5. Save regularly

- Google Sheets
- Notion templates
